Key Insights:

Sugar Cookie generated the highest sales revenue among all cookie categories.
Sugar Cookie was also the most profitable product, making it the top-performing cookie type.
Jaipur recorded the highest sales contribution compared to other cities.
The Wholesale channel generated the largest share of total sales.
Total sales exceeded ₹7.83 million, while total profit reached ₹2.36 million.
The dataset consisted of 2,000 orders with a total quantity sold of 313,452 units.
Monthly sales trends showed consistent performance throughout the year, indicating stable customer demand.
Sales were distributed across multiple cookie categories, reducing dependency on a single product.



Conclusion:

This dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of cookie sales performance by product category,
city, and sales channel. Using Excel Pivot Tables, Pivot Charts, and KPI cards,the analysis highlights key revenue drivers,
profitable products, and regional performance, helping businesses make data-driven decisions.
